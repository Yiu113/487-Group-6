Call for Airway Expert Help

If you suspect airway compromise or encounter difficulties with airway management in a laryngectomy patient, it's crucial to seek assistance from an airway expert.

General Information

Airway experts are skilled professionals trained in managing complex airway situations. They can provide valuable guidance and interventions to ensure optimal patient outcomes.

Contact Information

If available, refer to your institution's airway management team or contact the nearest anesthesia provider for assistance. In emergency situations, dial your institution's emergency code or call emergency medical services (EMS).
