Assess Laryngectomy Stoma Patency

It's important to assess the patency of the laryngectomy stoma to ensure proper airway management.

Many laryngectomy stomas will not have a tube present.

If present, remove any humidification cover or inner cannula.

Do not remove a tracheoesophageal puncture (TEP) prosthesis.
