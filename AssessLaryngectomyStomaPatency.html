Laryngectomy Emergency Airway Management

Created by Richard S. Lomax, Kirtan Patel, and Talen Benes

Assess laryngectomy stoma patency

Many laryngectomy stomas will NOT have a tube in stoma.

Remove humidification cover if present or Inner Cannula if trach is present.

Do NOT remove a tracheoesophageal puncture (TEP) prosthesis

Can you pass a suction catheter?
