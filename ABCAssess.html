Laryngectomy Emergency Airway Management

Created by Richard S. Lomax, Kirtan Patel, and Talen Benes

If the laryngectomy stoma is patent:

Perform tracheal suctioning

Consider partial obstruction

Ventilate via stoma

Continue with the ABCDE assessment.

ABCDE Assessment

ABCDE is a systematic approach used for the assessment and treatment of critically ill patients.
