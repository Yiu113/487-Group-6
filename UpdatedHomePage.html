Emergency Laryngectomy Management

Laryngectomy patients have an end stoma and cannot be ventilated or oxygenated via the mouth or nose.

These patients have a wrist bracelet that says "Neck breather"

Primary Emergency Oxygenation

Laryngectomy stoma ventilation via Infant size 2 face mask applied to stoma

Secondary Emergency Oxygenation

Attempt intubation of laryngectomy stoma

Size 4 DCT trach/ 6.0 cuffed ETT

Consider Fiberoptic scope/ Bougie/Airway exchange catheter

Go to Airway Expert
